Welcome to Apsara Bakers

Apsara is a brand with 20 years of experience in the food business specialising in cakes, pastries, puddings, breads, biscuits/cookies, dry cakes & snacks. Mr. Sachin Arora boarded the business to expand and unfold the journey towards success. We are responsible of making your occasions sweeter.

Apsara has consistently set itself the goal of defining the parameters of ‘dessertarian’ culture. We aim to bring the best flavours with exceptional quality and an eating experience par excellence. Our specialisation includes old school recipes with contemporary touch.

Our major aim is to popularize the Indian Delicacies with our cakes and chocolates. We intend to keep the rich aromatic flavours and mouth-watering appeal to all our cuisines. Our mission is to provide unique blend of cuisines that will help you taste the vast flavours under the same roof.
